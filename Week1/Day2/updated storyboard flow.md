2. HOW TO PLAY

•	Read the question carefully
•	Choose the safest answer
•	Earn rewards and coins
•	Protect your cyber world
•	Complete all levels to win

3. CHOOSE YOUR HERO

Player chooses a cyber ninja hero:
•	Misha 🥷 (Girl Cyber Ninja)

4. IN-GAME QUESTION SCREEN

•	Cyber security question appears
•	Timer starts (15 seconds)
•	Player has 3 lives ❤️
•	Reward and coins shown
•	Progress bar updates after every level
Example Question: “What does OTP stand for?”

5. RIGHT & WRONG EFFECTS

Correct Answer ✅

•	Character becomes happy 😄
•	Reward increases
•	Coins increase
•	Green success effect appears

Wrong Answer ❌

•	Character becomes angry 😡
•	One life is lost
•	Correct answer shown

Time Up ⏰

•	Character becomes sad 😭
•	One life is lost

6. SCORE & PROGRESS

Player can see:
•	Reward money
•	Coins
•	Remaining lives
•	Timer
•	Level progress
